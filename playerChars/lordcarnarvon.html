Lord Carnarvon

George Edward Stanhope Molyneux Herbert, 5. Earl of Carnarvon

Lord, adventurer, round-the-world yachtsman, photographer, car maniac, scholar

More about Lord Carnarvon

Born on June 26th of 1866 in Highclere Castle he is part of the anglo-welsh dynasty Herbert and can look back on ancients back to King Edward III. and King Henry VII. As the only heir of his father Henry Herbert, 4th Earl of Carnarvon, he also wears the courtesy title Lord Porchester and was called "Porchy" by his family
Tought by home teachers he was already able at the age of ten to talk French to his mother and his educators
